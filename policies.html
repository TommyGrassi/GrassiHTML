Policies and State Guidelines

Financial Policies

1. Payment Methods: Credit cards, debit cards, and online payment systems are just a few of the different payment options accepted by NH Meals. Bitcoin is not accepted here. By using encrypted payment gateways, we give the security of our customers' financial information top priority.

2. Pricing and Discounts: Fair market value serves as the foundation for our transparent pricing. We provide affordable prices without sacrificing quality. To thank our devoted customers, we occasionally offer exclusive discounts and promotions on a few products.

3. Refunds and Cancellations: NH Meals has a refund policy in place for cancelled orders. If a cancellation is made within the allotted time, customers may ask for a refund. It might take a few business days for the refund to appear in the customer's account after it has been processed using the original payment method.

Delivery Policies

1. Delivery Options: In order to guarantee that our customers receive their orders on time, NH Meals provides convenient delivery options. For urgent orders, we offer both standard and expedited delivery.

2. Delivery Areas: Currently, we cover the whole of New Hampshire. However, there might not be many delivery options in some isolated areas. During the checkout process, customers can check whether delivery is available to their specific location.

3. Delivery Charges: Depending on the order's value, the destination, and the delivery method selected, delivery fees might be assessed. When checking out, the applicable delivery costs are conspicuously displayed so that customers can review and confirm them before placing their orders.

4. Order Tracking: Order tracking functionality is provided by NH Meals to ensure a seamless experience. Through our website or by getting in touch with our customer support staff, customers can easily track their orders.

Return Policy

1. Eligibility: For eligible products, NH Meals offers a hassle-free return policy. Customers have 30 days from the date of purchase to start a return. The product must be in its original packaging and condition in order to be returned.

2. Return Process: TCustomers can start a return by getting in touch with our customer support staff or filling out a return request form on our website. In addition, returns via mail are accepted. A customer service agent will email you a shipping label so you can return your product if you want us to handle the return for you. Customers will be led through the return process by our staff, who will also offer any necessary instructions and shipping labels. After the activity date, services are not refundable.

3. Refund or Exchange: Following receipt of the returned item and confirmation of its eligibility, NH Meals will carry out the requested exchange or refund. Exchanges are contingent on the availability of the product, and refunds are given using the original payment method.

4. Exceptions: Due to hygiene or customization concerns, some products, such as perishable goods or personalized items, may not be returnable. Customers are urged to review the return policy for each individual product or get in touch with our customer support for more details.
